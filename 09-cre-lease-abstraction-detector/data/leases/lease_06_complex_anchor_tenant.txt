ANCHOR TENANT RETAIL LEASE AGREEMENT
(Negotiated form; supersedes ICSC template)

LANDLORD:  Crossroads Power Center Owner, L.P.
TENANT:    HomeWorks Hardware Co., a Delaware corporation
           ("Anchor" hereunder)

PROPERTY:  Crossroads Power Center
           4500 Crossroads Pkwy, Phoenix, AZ 85027

PREMISES:  Approximately 92,400 rentable square feet, comprising the
           full Building A and exclusive use of the loading area
           depicted on Exhibit B-1.

TERM:      Initial Term: One hundred eighty (180) full calendar months,
           commencing on March 1, 2026 ("Commencement Date") and
           expiring on February 28, 2041. Tenant shall have four (4)
           successive renewal options of five (5) years each, on the
           same terms (with rent reset to fair market rent at each
           renewal).

BASE RENT: Sixteen Dollars ($16.00) per rentable square foot per annum
           in Year 1, payable in equal monthly installments.

ESCALATION (Section 5.2): Annual Base Rent shall increase by ten percent
           (10%) at the start of Year 6, and ten percent (10%) at the
           start of Year 11. No annual escalation between resets.
           [Stepped escalation pattern.]

CAM (Section 7):
           Tenant shall pay Tenant's Pro Rata Share of Common Area
           Maintenance ("CAM") in accordance with Exhibit D. Tenant's
           Pro Rata Share is fixed at twenty-three and one-half percent
           (23.5%). Controllable CAM is capped at five dollars and
           seventy-five cents ($5.75) per RSF per annum in Year 1, with
           annual increases of the lesser of (a) the percentage increase
           in CPI-U or (b) four percent (4.0%). Real estate taxes,
           insurance, and utilities are uncapped. Base year for CAM
           reconciliation is calendar year 2026.

CO-TENANCY (Section 11): If, at any time after the Commencement Date,
           the Center fails to maintain at least three (3) of the
           following four (4) co-anchor tenants — (i) a national grocery
           operator with at least 35,000 RSF; (ii) Best Buy or
           equivalent national consumer electronics retailer; (iii)
           Ross, T.J. Maxx, or equivalent national off-price retailer;
           (iv) a national pharmacy with at least 12,000 RSF — and such
           failure continues for one hundred eighty (180) consecutive
           days, Tenant may, at Tenant's option, (a) pay Alternate Rent
           equal to the lesser of two percent (2.0%) of Gross Sales or
           Base Rent, until the co-tenancy failure is cured; or (b) if
           the failure continues for an additional twelve (12) months
           thereafter, terminate this Lease on ninety (90) days'
           written notice (the "Kick-Out Right").

KICK-OUT (Section 12): In addition to the co-tenancy Kick-Out Right
           in Section 11, Tenant shall have the right to terminate
           this Lease at the end of the seventh (7th) Lease Year if
           Tenant's Gross Sales from the Premises in the immediately
           preceding twelve-month period are less than Twenty-Two
           Million Dollars ($22,000,000) (the "Sales-Based Kick-Out").
           Tenant must give written notice of such termination no
           later than the last day of the eighty-fourth (84th) full
           calendar month of the Term.

EXCLUSIVITY (Section 13): During the Term, Landlord shall not lease,
           sublease, or permit any space within the Center to be used
           primarily for the retail sale of (a) hardware, (b) home-
           improvement supplies, (c) lumber, or (d) paint, in any
           premises larger than five thousand (5,000) RSF. Excluded:
           hardware-and-paint sections of any grocery anchor under
           1,500 RSF.

ASSIGNMENT: Tenant may assign this Lease to (a) any affiliate; (b) any
           successor by merger, consolidation, or sale of substantially
           all assets; or (c) any third party with Landlord's prior
           written consent, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.

OPTIONS:   No expansion right. No ROFO. No ROFR.

This Lease is dated as of January 22, 2026.
