OFFICE LEASE AGREEMENT
BOMA-Standard Form, January 2024 Edition

LANDLORD:  Lakeside Tower Investors, LLC
TENANT:    Veridian Health Analytics, Inc.

PROPERTY:  Lakeside Tower, Floors 14-15
           600 Wacker Drive, Chicago, IL 60606

PREMISES:  Approximately 38,200 rentable square feet, comprising the
           full 14th floor (19,100 RSF) and full 15th floor (19,100 RSF).

TERM:      Ninety-six (96) full calendar months, commencing on
           February 1, 2026 ("Commencement Date") and expiring on
           January 31, 2034.

BASE RENT: Forty-Six Dollars ($46.00) per rentable square foot per annum
           in Year 1, payable in equal monthly installments.

ESCALATION: Base Rent shall increase by two and one-half percent (2.5%)
           per annum on each anniversary of the Commencement Date.

OPERATING EXPENSES: Tenant shall pay its Pro Rata Share of increases
           in Operating Expenses over the Base Year (calendar year
           2026). Controllable Operating Expenses shall not increase by
           more than five percent (5%) per annum on a cumulative,
           compounding basis.

PERMITTED USE: General office and ancillary laboratory use.

OPTIONS:   One (1) renewal option of five (5) years at then-fair-market
           rent, with twelve (12) months' prior written notice.

ASSIGNMENT: Tenant may not assign or sublet without Landlord's prior
           written consent, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.

This Lease is dated as of December 18, 2025.

Date: December 18, 2025

Re: Lakeside Tower Office Lease — Veridian Health Analytics, Inc.

Reference is made to that certain Office Lease Agreement dated as of
December 18, 2025 (the "Lease") between Lakeside Tower Investors, LLC
("Landlord") and Veridian Health Analytics, Inc. ("Tenant"). Capitalized
terms used but not defined herein shall have the meanings set forth in
the Lease.

The parties agree as follows:

1. RIGHT OF FIRST OFFER (ROFO).  During the Term, before Landlord
   markets to a third party any rentable space on Floor 13 of the
   Building (the "ROFO Space"), Landlord shall first deliver to Tenant
   a written notice ("ROFO Notice") setting forth the proposed economic
   terms (rent, escalation, term length, tenant improvement allowance).
   Tenant shall have fifteen (15) business days after receipt of the
   ROFO Notice to elect, by written notice to Landlord, to lease the
   ROFO Space on the terms set forth in the ROFO Notice. If Tenant
   does not so elect within such fifteen (15) business day period,
   Landlord shall be free to lease the ROFO Space to any third party
   on terms no more favorable to such third party than those set forth
   in the ROFO Notice for a period of nine (9) months thereafter.

2. PARKING.  Tenant shall be entitled to two (2) reserved garage
   stalls per 1,000 RSF of the Premises, at the prevailing monthly rate.

3. SIGNAGE.  Tenant shall be entitled to exterior signage on the
   north face of the Building, subject to Landlord's reasonable
   consent and applicable City of Chicago sign-code approvals.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY.  This Side Letter and its terms are confidential
   between the parties.

This Side Letter is executed as of the date first written above.
