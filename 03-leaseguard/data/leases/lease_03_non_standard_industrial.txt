INDUSTRIAL LEASE AGREEMENT
(Non-standard form drafted by Tenant counsel; superseded ICSC template)

LANDLORD:  Pacific Logistics Properties, a California limited partnership
TENANT:    MeridianFlow Distribution, Inc.

PROPERTY:  Bay Park Logistics Center, Building D
           28100 Cabot Road, Hayward, CA 94545

PREMISES:  The "Demised Premises" comprises approximately 142,500 rentable
           square feet of warehouse and 6,200 RSF of attached office,
           together with exclusive use of forty-two (42) dock-high doors
           and two (2) drive-in doors.

TERM:      The Initial Term shall commence on the date that is sixty (60)
           days following the date Landlord delivers the Premises in the
           Delivery Condition (such date, the "Commencement Date"), and
           shall continue for a period of eighty-four (84) calendar months
           thereafter. The parties anticipate, but do not warrant, a
           Commencement Date of September 15, 2025.

BASE RENT: Initial annual rent shall equal Twelve Dollars and Sixty Cents
           ($12.60) per rentable square foot of warehouse space and Twenty-
           Eight Dollars and Zero Cents ($28.00) per rentable square foot
           of office space, computed on a blended basis.

ESCALATION (Section 4.3): Commencing on the first day of the thirteenth
           (13th) full calendar month of the Term and on each anniversary
           thereafter, annual Base Rent shall increase by the lesser of
           (i) the percentage change, if any, in the Consumer Price Index
           for All Urban Consumers (CPI-U) for the San Francisco-Oakland-
           Hayward MSA over the trailing twelve months, or (ii) four and
           one-half percent (4.5%); provided that in no event shall the
           annual increase be less than two percent (2.0%).

           [That is: CPI-indexed, floor 2.0%, cap 4.5%.]

CAM / NNN: This is a triple-net lease. Tenant shall pay all Operating
           Expenses, Real Estate Taxes, and Insurance directly or as
           reimbursement to Landlord. Controllable CAM is capped at the
           greater of (a) four percent (4%) over the prior year's
           controllable CAM or (b) seven dollars ($7.00) per RSF per
           annum, base year 2025. Uncontrollable CAM (taxes, insurance,
           snow removal, utilities) is uncapped.

AMENDMENT NO. 1 (executed at lease signing, embedded herein):
           Section 4.3 is hereby modified solely with respect to the first
           Lease Year: notwithstanding anything in Section 4.3, no
           escalation shall apply during the first twelve months of the
           Term.

AMENDMENT NO. 2 (executed at lease signing, embedded herein):
           Tenant shall have the right, but not the obligation, to lease
           the contiguous 38,400 RSF in Building D-2 ("Expansion Space")
           upon ninety (90) days' notice given at any time during months
           24-48 of the Initial Term, at the then-prevailing Base Rent
           per RSF for the Premises. This is a Right of First Offer
           (ROFO), not a Right of First Refusal.

PERMITTED USE: Warehousing, distribution, light assembly, and ancillary
           office.

This Lease, including embedded Amendments No. 1 and No. 2, is dated as
of June 30, 2025.
