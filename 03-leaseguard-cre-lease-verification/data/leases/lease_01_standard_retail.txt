RETAIL LEASE AGREEMENT
ICSC-Standard Form, March 2024 Edition

LANDLORD:  Northbridge Retail Holdings, LLC
TENANT:    Brewmoor Coffee Roasters, Inc., a Delaware corporation

PROPERTY:  Northbridge Town Center, Suite 140
           1450 Market Crossing Blvd, Charlotte, NC 28203

PREMISES:  Approximately 2,400 rentable square feet on the ground floor.

TERM:      Sixty (60) full calendar months, commencing on April 1, 2025
           ("Commencement Date") and expiring on March 31, 2030.

BASE RENT: Forty Dollars ($40.00) per rentable square foot per annum,
           payable in equal monthly installments of $8,000.00.

ESCALATION: Base Rent shall increase by three percent (3.0%) per annum on
           each anniversary of the Commencement Date, compounding annually.

CAM:       Tenant shall pay Tenant's Pro Rata Share of Common Area
           Maintenance ("CAM") expenses. CAM cap of twelve dollars
           ($12.00) per rentable square foot per annum on controllable
           CAM, with a base year of 2025.

PERMITTED USE: Specialty coffee retail and ancillary food service.

OPTIONS:   None. No renewal options. No expansion rights. No right of
           first refusal.

CO-TENANCY: None.

EXCLUSIVITY: None.

ASSIGNMENT: Tenant may not assign this Lease without Landlord's prior
           written consent, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.

This Lease is entered into as of January 12, 2025.
