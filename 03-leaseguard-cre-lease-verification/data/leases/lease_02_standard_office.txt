OFFICE LEASE AGREEMENT
BOMA-Standard Form, January 2024 Edition

LANDLORD:  Hudson Yards Office Trust, L.P.
TENANT:    Argent Capital Advisors, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company

PROPERTY:  455 Hudson Yards, Floor 22, Suite 2200
           New York, NY 10001

PREMISES:  Approximately 11,800 rentable square feet, full Suite 2200.

TERM:      One hundred twenty (120) full calendar months, commencing on
           July 1, 2025 ("Commencement Date") and expiring on June 30, 2035.

BASE RENT: Eighty-Five Dollars ($85.00) per rentable square foot per annum
           in Year 1, payable in equal monthly installments.

ESCALATION: Base Rent shall increase in fixed annual steps as follows:
           Year 2: $87.55 PSF
           Year 3: $90.18 PSF
           Year 4: $92.88 PSF
           Year 5: $95.67 PSF
           Year 6: $98.54 PSF
           Year 7: $101.50 PSF
           Year 8: $104.54 PSF
           Year 9: $107.68 PSF
           Year 10: $110.91 PSF

OPERATING EXPENSES: Tenant shall pay its Pro Rata Share of increases in
           Operating Expenses over the Base Year (calendar year 2025).
           No cap on controllable expenses.

PERMITTED USE: General office use.

OPTIONS:   One (1) renewal option of five (5) years at then-fair-market
           rent, with twelve (12) months' prior written notice.

ASSIGNMENT: Tenant may not assign or sublet without Landlord's prior
           written consent, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.

SNDA:      Tenant has executed a Subordination, Non-Disturbance, and
           Attornment Agreement attached as Exhibit C.

This Lease is dated as of May 5, 2025.
